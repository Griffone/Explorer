Welcome to Technomunk design!
This page is used to develop, refine and showcase Technomunk design. The design is centered around simple, yet striking visuals with a strong identity. Core principals of Technomunk design are: plenty of spacing, showing rather than telling, clean and sharp lines. A minimalist look can be hard to achieve, but can be used in a wide variety of different applications, from web-page design (such as this one) to app-skin to document formatting principals.
Design criteria are:
  • High legibility.
  • Mobile-friendliness.
  • Ease of quick-parsing.
  • Minimal requirements for the content.
